Welcome to the Australian Mobile Phone Usage While Driving Dashboard

This dashboard provides comprehensive information on Australian drivers caught using their mobile phones in 2023. Through interactive visualisations, we explore key patterns and correlations in the data.

Key Insights You Can Explore:

  • Compare the prevalence of camera-issued versus police-issued fines across different jurisdictions
  • Analyse the total number of fines issued per jurisdiction throughout 2023
  • Investigate the relationship between road deaths and fines issued in each jurisdiction
